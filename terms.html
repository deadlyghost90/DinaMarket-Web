gavel Legal Compliance Notice: DinaMarket Pakistan is legally operated as a decentralized peer-to-peer advertising and catalog directory platform in compliance with Pakistan’s Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA) 2016.

1. Platform Architecture & Decentralized Operations

DinaMarket Pakistan is not a commercial merchant, physical storefront, or standard e-commerce counter. We provide decentralized web software facilitation under regional rules. All transactions, catalog uploads, prices, store layouts, and product counts are managed strictly by independent third-party entrepreneurs ("Merchants") in Pakistan.

2. Peer-to-Peer Wallet Protected Direct Payments

Financial settlement is based purely on a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) model. Buyers settle balances directly with the chosen seller using verified Pakistani mobile wallet infrastructure including:

  • EasyPaisa Wallet transfers
  • JazzCash Wallet transfers

Sellers display their authenticated account coordinates dynamically. Every buyer must supply a valid 12-digit transaction ID (TID) confirmation code generated natively by the corresponding mobile wallet application during checkout. DinaMarket Pakistan maintains zero escrow, handles no standard buyer refunds, and acts strictly as a non-custodial cryptographic audit ledger for transactional verifications.

3. Success-Based Setup & Merchant Pricing Thresholds

Basic register operations, storefront listings, and boosting algorithms remain 100% free for all local Pakistani merchants and startups. However, once a Merchant successfully achieves a threshold of 5 to 10 completed client transactions through our portal, the Merchant agrees to register for our success-based service support. This includes a small, flat transaction percentage service fee to support ongoing web server maintenance and bandwidth support.

4. Delivery Limitations & Self-Pickup Logistics

DinaMarket Pakistan does not operate a third-party rider squad, delivery network, or physical dispatch fleet. All logistics, delivery coordinates, estimated arrival thresholds, packaging, and pick-up schemes are governed entirely by:

  • Customer Self-Pickup from the seller’s verified physical hub address, or
  • Merchant-Owned/Private Delivery services handled directly by the individual store staff.

5. Product Integrity and Digital Reviews

Any attempts to inject fake product listings, fraudulent review strings, artificial star rating manipulation, or non-authentic TID confirmations constitute a material violation of these Terms. Under PECA guidelines, all users agree to supply real-time verified profile data, authentic contact phone numbers, and legitimate transaction details.
